Work

    Delta Investment Management (2014 - 2015)

    My first job was at Delta Investment Management working under my dad and his business partner. I wrote Excel VBA programs to retrieve historical stock data for securities and backtest strategies. The strategies we developed are still used today, and I occasionally do work for Delta to update old code.

    Southern Glazers Wine and Spirits (Sep 2017 - Dec 2018)

    My first job after graduating was working as a data analyst at Southern Glazer's Wine and Spirits. I made sales reports using SQL and VBA and wrote python/VBA scripts to automate invoice filing. I really enjoyed learning SQL and writing scripts, but disliked report making. I was put on a project to build a database of sales discounts by manually entering in thousands of non standardized deals into a SQL table. I decided it was time to get a new job.

    I was searching for similar positions, until my friend Kiarash Mavandad called to tell me about an opening at his company, Badger Meter, for a software engineering position. I never considered working as a software engineer because I lacked the qualifications, but his call opened up my mind to the possibility. I applied and was rejected due to my lack of knowledge and experience, but I began working towards becoming a software engineer. I spent the next 2 months coding as much as I could. My hard work paid off when Badger Meter decided to re-interview me and gave me the job. I moved out of my parents place to live in San Jose and start my new career as a Software Engineer. I was beyond excited!

    Badger Meter (Dec 2018 - Current)

    I have been working for Badger Meter since December 2018. I worked as an intern for 3 months and was then hired full time after my internship. I am currently a Senior Software Engineer and team lead for the Analytics team, which does analytics on sensor data from water meters. I work on back end development in Python and DevOps in AWS. I really enjoy the work and have made thousands of contributions to the Github Badger Meter Organization.

Hobbies

  • Chess

    One of my passions outside of work is chess. My dad taught me the rules when I was young. I didn't play much growing up, but started playing in the college dorms when students set up boards in the hallways. I created my chess.com account the summer after my freshman year and played my first online game on July 2, 2014. I worked my way up from a 600 rating to a peak rating of 2374 and went from the 5th percentile of players on the site to the 99.9th percentile. I started playing tournament chess in 2019 at the Kolty Chess club. I have played about 50 over the board games and have a rating of 2064. My goal is to become a National Master by reaching a rating of 2200.

    Here is a win I had against GM Gata Kamsky in a simultaneous exhibition. Gata was playing several players at once and wanted to go to bed so the timing was on my side! I found an unusual tactic in the opening which gave me a winning advantage. Gata came close to recovering, but I was able to hold on to my advantage long enough to win the game. I have been a fan of Gata since I started playing chess. Game Source .
